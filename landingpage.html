The Employment Integration Challenge

Despite progressive policies in Rwanda, refugees face significant barriers to meaningful employment that match their skills and qualifications.

Systemic Barriers to Workforce Integration

  • Language proficiency requirements (Kinyarwanda, English, French)
  • Limited access to local professional networks and job markets
  • Challenges in verifying foreign credentials and work experience
  • Employer unfamiliarity with refugee skills and potential
  • Cultural differences in workplace expectations and practices

These systemic challenges result in significant underemployment among refugee populations, despite Rwanda's supportive legal framework that permits refugee employment.
