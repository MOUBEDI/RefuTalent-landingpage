Our Digital Inclusion Pathway

How we're transforming refugee employment through technology

1

Talent Sourcing & Onboarding

Refugees create comprehensive digital profiles showcasing their skills, experience, and qualifications through our accessible platform.

2

Upskilling & Vetting

Our refugee-led team provides culturally-relevant soft skills training, language support, and thorough vetting to ensure job readiness.

3

Matching Algorithm

Advanced algorithms connect candidates with employers based on skills, experience, and cultural fit—not refugee status.
