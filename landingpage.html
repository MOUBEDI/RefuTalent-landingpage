Connecting Skilled Refugees with Meaningful Employment

RefuLink bridges the gap between talented refugees and employers in Rwanda, creating opportunities for dignified work and economic integration.

I'm an Employer I'm a Job Seeker

The Challenge Refugees Face

Despite their skills and qualifications, refugees in Rwanda often struggle to find employment due to systemic barriers.

Barriers to Employment

  • Language differences (Kinyarwanda, English, French)
  • Lack of local professional networks
  • Difficulty verifying foreign credentials
  • Limited awareness among employers about refugee talents
  • Cultural differences in workplace expectations

These challenges lead to underemployment and wasted potential, despite Rwanda's progressive policies allowing refugees to work.

Our Innovative Solution

RefuLink addresses these challenges through a comprehensive platform that connects, verifies, and matches talent with opportunity.

How RefuLink Works

Our simple three-step process creates meaningful connections between refugees and employers.

1

Profile Creation

Refugees create detailed profiles with skills, experience, and qualifications. Our team verifies all information.

2

Smart Matching

Our algorithm matches refugee profiles with suitable job openings based on skills, experience, and location.

3

Job Placement

Employers connect with candidates, conduct interviews, and make hiring decisions with our support.
